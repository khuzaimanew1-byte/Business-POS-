# 🖱️ TOOLTIP HOVER BEHAVIOR (IMPORTANT UX FIX)

## 🎯 Goal:

Prevent accidental tooltip flicker and improve click accuracy.

---

## ⏱️ DELAYED TOOLTIP APPEARANCE

### Behavior:

* Tooltip must NOT appear instantly on hover
* Introduce a **small delay before showing tooltip**

### Timing:

* On hover → wait **200ms to 300ms**
* If cursor remains → show tooltip smoothly
* If cursor leaves before delay → do nothing

---

## 🎬 TOOLTIP DISAPPEAR BEHAVIOR

* On mouse leave → tooltip disappears immediately OR within very fast fade (100ms max)
* No lingering delay on exit

---

## 🎨 VISUAL STYLE

* Fade-in animation (not slide-heavy)
* Soft opacity transition
* No jitter or flicker
* Must feel stable during fast cursor movement

## ⚡ IMPACT
