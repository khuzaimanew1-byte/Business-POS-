BACKGROUND
The page background is plain black right now. Add a fixed 
canvas element behind everything. On load and on window 
resize, fill it with #04070a then paint three nebula clouds 
using radial gradients — first one in the upper-left area, 
deep teal color at around 5% opacity, spread about 40% of 
screen width. Second one lower-right, a barely warm neutral 
tone at 4% opacity. Third one near center, cool blue at 
under 4% opacity. All three should be so faint the eye 
feels depth without consciously seeing clouds. Finish with 
a vignette — radial gradient transparent at center fading 
to rgba(0,0,0,0.62) at the outer edges. This canvas never 
animates after the initial draw.
